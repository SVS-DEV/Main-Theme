SVS Prime Wireless: Convincing & Immersive Sound Goes Wireless.

SVS Prime Wireless delivers an uncompromised wireless audio solution with amazing sound quality and stunning dynamics to thrill even the most discerning listeners. Before SVS, wireless speakers offered good sound relative to their compact size or lifestyle-oriented design, but they couldn’t deliver a truly immersive listening experience. With SVS Prime Wireless, you get convincing, “like-being-there” sound quality from a powered speaker and integrated amplifier designed for the modern audiophile. Add in sophisticated features and unmatched versatility and Prime Wireless is ready to take the audio world by storm. Prime Wireless will be available late spring or early summer 2018.
